My experience

I live to satisfy my endless curiosity. I originally studied industrial design; however, since my childhood I had a softspot for technology. This led me to change my career path to do what I enjoy. I have always been fascinated by video game mechanics, which led me to explore the indie developer scene. I eventually started making my own projects in Game Maker and RPG maker around 2009 inspired by The Legend of Zelda, and Super Mario Bros and Earthbound. I studied my bachelor and masters degree in Medialogy at Aalborg University CPH, where I specialized in virtual and augmented reality. Did my internship at the Virtual Learning Lab at Copenhagen university, where I was hired immediately after. I have many years of experience working in Unity 3D, C# and Blender.
